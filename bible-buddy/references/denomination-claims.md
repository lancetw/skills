## Seventh-day Adventist (adventist-theology)

**Claim 1: Investigative Judgment began in 1844 (Daniel 8:14)**
- Scripture: Daniel 8:14 ("2300 evenings-mornings, then the sanctuary shall be restored")
- 1C Reality: Hebrew erev boqer refers to tamid sacrifice cycles; context is Antiochus IV's desecration of the earthly temple (~167-164 BCE), not a heavenly judgment in 1844
- Key Evidence: v'nitzdak (root ts-d-q) means "restored to rightness," not "cleansed" (taher); 1844 calculation derives from William Miller (~1840s), not any 1C Jewish reading

**Claim 2: Ellen White as end-time prophet**
- Scripture: Deuteronomy 18:15-22 (prophet like Moses)
- 1C Reality: Torah's prophet test (Deut 18:20-22) requires 100% accuracy; Isaiah 8:20 anchors all prophecy to Torah/te'udah
- Key Evidence: kamoni ("like me") = from within Israel's covenant community; navi test is falsifiability, not charismatic authority

**Claim 3: Saturday Sabbath as salvific requirement**
- Scripture: Exodus 20:8-11, Genesis 2:2-3
- 1C Reality: Shabbat is a covenant sign (ot) between YHWH and Israel (Exodus 31:13-17), not a universal salvation test
- Key Evidence: Hebrew ot (sign) marks covenant membership; 1C debate was about how to keep Shabbat, not whether it determines salvation

**Claim 4: Soul sleep (no conscious afterlife)**
- Scripture: Ecclesiastes 9:5 ("the dead know nothing")
- 1C Reality: Ecclesiastes is wisdom literature reflecting human observation "under the sun"; 1C Judaism had diverse afterlife views (Pharisees affirmed resurrection, Sadducees denied it)
- Key Evidence: Hebrew hevel framework = life observed from limited human perspective; not a doctrinal statement on post-mortem consciousness

## Calvinism (calvinism-theology)

**Claim: TULIP -- individual predestination to salvation/damnation**
- Scripture: Romans 9:11-13 (Jacob/Esau), Ephesians 1:4-5
- 1C Reality: Romans 9's "Jacob and Esau" = two nations (goyim), not two individuals' eternal destinies; Paul's argument spans chs. 9-11, climaxing in "God will have mercy on all" (11:32)
- Key Evidence: Hebrew bachar (choose) = covenantal election of a people for a mission, not individual predestination; shenei goyim (Gen 25:23) = two nations; sane (Mal 1:3) = "not chosen for covenant role," not personal hatred
- Anachronism: TULIP formulated at Synod of Dort (1618-1619); yetzer (good/evil inclination) framework, not total depravity; Deut 30:19 commands Israel to "choose" (bachar) life

## Roman Catholic (catholic-theology)

**Claim 1: Immaculate Conception of Mary**
- Scripture: Luke 1:28 (kecharitomene, "highly favored")
- 1C Reality: Greek perfect passive = "having been graced," Mary is recipient not source; same root (charitoo) applies to all believers in Eph 1:6
- Key Evidence: "Original sin" is Augustine's concept (4-5C); 1C Jewish yetzer framework has no inherited sin to be "immune" from; dogma defined 1854

**Claim 2: Perpetual Virginity**
- Scripture: Luke 1:27 (parthenos)
- 1C Reality: Matt 1:25 heos hou ("until") implies marital relations after birth; Matt 13:55-56 names four brothers + sisters; Greek adelphos used, not anepsios (cousin, cf. Col 4:10)
- Key Evidence: Protoevangelium of James (2C apocryphal) is earliest source; formally defined 553 CE

**Claim 3: Theotokos (Mother of God)**
- Scripture: Luke 1:43 ("mother of my Lord")
- 1C Reality: Council of Ephesus 431 CE defined this as Christological (about Jesus' nature), not Mariological; 1C Jewish category is mission-bearer like Gideon (Judg 6:12)
- Key Evidence: Jesus himself redirects Marian veneration (Mark 3:33-35, Luke 11:27-28)

**Claim 4: Marian intercession/prayer**
- Scripture: (No direct scripture cited)
- 1C Reality: 1 Tim 2:5 -- heis mesites (one mediator); Deut 18:10-12 forbids communication with the dead; no NT instance of prayer to Mary or any deceased saint

## Chinese Three-Self / CCP Theology (ccp-theology)

**Claim 1: Acts 2:44-45 = Communism**
- Scripture: Acts 2:44-45 ("all things in common")
- 1C Reality: Acts 5:4 -- Peter affirms private property rights ("wasn't it yours?"); the sin was lying, not keeping property
- Key Evidence: Greek koina/koinonia = voluntary partnership; Torah protects property (lo tignov, lo tachmod -- Exod 20:15,17); Jubilee (Yovel) restores, not redistributes

**Claim 2: Exodus = Class Revolution**
- Scripture: Exodus 3:7-10
- 1C Reality: Agent is YHWH ("I saw, I came down, I rescue"), not the proletariat; purpose is covenant worship (ta'avdun et ha'Elohim), not classless society
- Key Evidence: Hebrew ga'al = kinship redemption; avad = transfer from serving Pharaoh to serving YHWH; framework is berit (covenant), not class struggle

**Claim 3: John 8:32 "truth" = Marxism**
- Scripture: John 8:32 ("the truth will set you free")
- 1C Reality: "Truth" (aletheia/emet) = YHWH's faithful character; "freedom" defined in 8:34 as freedom from sin (doulos tes hamartias), not political/economic liberation
- Key Evidence: Hebrew emet (aleph-mem-nun root) = reliable, steadfast; Mishnah Avot 6:2 -- "only Torah students are truly free"; 1800-year anachronism to read in Marx (1848)

## Charismatic / Prosperity Gospel (charismatic-theology)

**Claim 1: Tongues = sole evidence of Spirit baptism**
- Scripture: Acts 2:4
- 1C Reality: Pentecost tongues were real human languages (dialektos, Acts 2:8); Paul explicitly denies universality (1 Cor 12:30 with me expecting "no"); Acts 8:14-17 has no tongues
- Key Evidence: Shavuot/Sinai tradition -- God's voice in 70 languages (Midr. Tanchuma); Joel 3:1 uses nava (prophesy), not "speak in tongues"; doctrine originates from Azusa Street Revival (1906)

**Claim 2: Mark 16:17-18 (snake handling, poison drinking)**
- Scripture: Mark 16:17-18
- 1C Reality: Mark 16:9-20 is absent from Sinaiticus and Vaticanus (earliest MSS); Eusebius confirms "accurate copies" end at 16:8; promissory language, not prescriptive command
- Key Evidence: Textual criticism = later addition; 1C Jewish halakhah/aggadah distinction -- narrative is not normative

**Claim 3: Tithing guarantees personal wealth (Malachi 3:10)**
- Scripture: Malachi 3:10
- 1C Reality: Addressee is post-exilic Israel as a nation (ha-goy kullo); "blessing" is agricultural (rain, locust protection, Mal 3:11); covenantal conditional language (Deut 28)
- Key Evidence: Hebrew berakhah operates within berit framework; ma'aser supports Levites/temple, not personal ROI

**Claim 4: 3 John 2 = God wants everyone rich**
- Scripture: 3 John 1:2
- 1C Reality: Standard 1C epistolary greeting formula (like "I hope this finds you well"); euodousthai = "go well on the road," not financial prosperity
- Key Evidence: Oxyrhynchus papyri show identical health wishes in secular letters; addressed to one person (Gaius), not a theological universal

## Evangelical / Church Language (church-language)

**Claim: John 3:16 teaches Trinity, Original Sin, Penal Substitution**
- Scripture: John 3:14-21
- 1C Reality: Bronze serpent analogy (Num 21:8-9) = "look and live" (trust), not substitutionary punishment; monogenes = "unique" (not "begotten"); "believe" (pisteuo) = emunah (covenant faithfulness demonstrated in action)
- Key Evidence: Yetzer (inclination) not original sin; kafar = purify/restore covenant, not penal substitution; Trinity defined 325-381 CE (Nicaea/Constantinople); vv. 20-21 emphasize erga (works/deeds)

## Confucianism (confucianism)

**Claim: Biblical ethics = Confucian ethics (Golden Rule, filial piety, ren = love)**
- Scripture: Matt 7:12, Exod 20:12, Lev 19:18
- 1C Reality: Surface parallels mask structural differences; biblical ethics are covenantal (YHWH acts first, humans respond), not humanistic self-cultivation
- Key Evidence: kabbed (honor parents) = covenant obligation anchored in land promise, not social order; ahavah = treaty-loyalty action verb (Amarna letters); Hillel's Golden Rule predates Jesus but anchored in Torah; ancestor worship (core to Confucian xiao) explicitly forbidden (Deut 18:10-12)

## Daoism (daoist-theology)

**Claim 1: Logos (John 1:1) = Dao**
- Scripture: John 1:1-5
- 1C Reality: Logos' Hebrew background is davar (YHWH's spoken word that acts); personal, volitional command vs. impersonal natural principle
- Key Evidence: Ps 33:6 bidvar YHWH = "by YHWH's word"; translation to Chinese "Dao" (1919 CUV) creates the illusion of connection

**Claim 2: Tohu va-vohu (Gen 1:2) = Wuji/Taiji**
- Scripture: Genesis 1:1-3
- 1C Reality: Tohu va-vohu = unformed state awaiting God's ordering speech; true dialogue partner is ANE myth (Enuma Elish), not Daodejing
- Key Evidence: Chinese "hundun" carries Daoist associations; Hebrew describes a personal God's step-by-step ordering, not emanation

**Claim 3: Ruach = Qi**
- Scripture: Genesis 1:2, 2:7
- 1C Reality: Ruach = YHWH's personal, volitional action/presence; n'shamah (Gen 2:7) = God deliberately blows breath; not impersonal energy
- Key Evidence: Ps 104:29-30 -- "Your ruach" (possessive) given and withdrawn by YHWH; humans are passive recipients, not cultivators

## Devotional Misreading (devotional-misread)

**Claim 1: Isaiah 43:2 = personal promise of safety**
- Scripture: Isaiah 43:1-7
- 1C Reality: Addressee is Ya'akov/Yisra'el (nation); "water/fire" = geopolitical obstacles (Euphrates crossing, imperial oppression); vv. 3-4 name Egypt, Cush, Seba as ransom -- real nations, not metaphors
- Key Evidence: Second Isaiah (~540 BCE) addresses Babylonian exiles; berakhah is national covenant promise, not individual protection guarantee

**Claim 2: Jeremiah 29:11 = God's plan for your career**
- Scripture: Jeremiah 29:10-14
- 1C Reality: Addressed to Judean exiles in Babylon; "70 years" = specific geopolitical timeline; "plans of shalom" = national restoration, not individual life planning
- Key Evidence: v.14 "gather you from all nations" = physical return from exile; machashavot shalom = covenant thoughts of wholeness for the nation

**Claim 3: Philippians 4:13 = I can achieve anything**
- Scripture: Philippians 4:10-13
- 1C Reality: Paul writing from prison about contentment in both abundance and need; "all things" = endure any circumstance
- Key Evidence: Greek en to endynamounti me = "in the one who empowers me" -- context is suffering and deprivation, not success

## Easter Theology (easter-theology)

**Claim: Easter is the Christian celebration of resurrection**
- Scripture: Leviticus 23:5-14, 1 Corinthians 5:7, 15:20
- 1C Reality: Resurrection is embedded in Pesach/Matzot/Bikkurim (Passover/Unleavened Bread/Firstfruits); Paul calls Christ "our Pesach lamb" and "firstfruits" (aparchE = reshit); Quartodeciman controversy shows early believers commemorated on Nisan 14
- Key Evidence: Nicaea 325 CE formally severed from Jewish calendar; Easter/Ishtar connection is false (Eostre = Germanic dawn goddess, unrelated to Mesopotamian Ishtar); rabbits (1678 CE) and eggs (medieval Lent) have zero 1C basis

## Taiwan Folk Religion (folk-religion)

**Claim 1: Mazu = Virgin Mary / Proverbs 31 woman**
- Scripture: Proverbs 31:10-31
- 1C Reality: Eshet chayil is a human woman praised for yir'at-YHWH (fearing YHWH); she is a worshiper, not worshiped; deifying humans violates Exod 20:3-4
- Key Evidence: Acrostic poem = family ethics education; Mary veneration developed 4-5C, not 1C

**Claim 2: Guanyin = Holy Spirit (parakletos)**
- Scripture: John 14:16-17, 26
- 1C Reality: Parakletos = legal advocate in bet din; ruach ha-kodesh = YHWH's own action/presence, not an independent entity; John 14:17 "world cannot receive him" = exclusive
- Key Evidence: Hosea 13:4 "no savior besides me"; parakletos operates within berit, not universally

**Claim 3: Karma = "reap what you sow" (Galatians 6:7)**
- Scripture: Galatians 6:7-8
- 1C Reality: Personal God (theos) administers consequences within covenant; YHWH can forgive (hesed, Exod 34:6-7); Job 42:7 rebukes mechanical retribution logic
- Key Evidence: Karma = impersonal automatic law; nefesh is whole living person, not reincarnating soul; teshuvah (repentance) breaks the cycle -- karma has no forgiveness mechanism

## Gnosticism (gnostic-theology)

**Claim: John's Gospel is a Gnostic text (light/dark dualism, secret knowledge)**
- Scripture: John 1:1-5, 8:23, 17:3
- 1C Reality: John 1:1 echoes bereshit (Gen 1:1), not emanation; light/dark is standard prophetic imagery (Isa 9:1, 60:1-2, Ps 119:105); John 1:14 "the Word became sarx (flesh)" is anti-Gnostic -- Gnostics held the divine cannot become material
- Key Evidence: Logos = davar/memra/hokhmah tradition; 1:3 "all things made through him" = positive creation theology, opposite of Demiurge doctrine; hokhmah "delights in bnei adam" (Prov 8:31) = loves material world

## Guan Gong Worship (guan-gong-theology)

**Claim 1: Guan Gong's loyalty (yi) = Micah 6:8 justice**
- Scripture: Micah 6:8
- 1C Reality: mishpat = structural covenant justice; hesed = YHWH's covenant loyalty; yi qi = interpersonal honor-loyalty; hatznea lekhet im elohekha = walk humbly with your God (not a human hero's virtue)
- Key Evidence: Micah 6:3-5 recounts Exodus -- the ethical demand responds to YHWH's prior saving action

**Claim 2: Guan Gong blesses business = Proverbs 10:22**
- Scripture: Proverbs 10:22
- 1C Reality: birkat YHWH = covenantal blessing within Torah obedience (Deut 28); not transactional (sacrifice -> reward); v.22b "without etzev" echoes Gen 3:16-17 curse
- Key Evidence: Micah 6:6-7 critiques transactional religion; YHWH's berakhah is non-manipulable

**Claim 3: Guan Gong's righteousness = Abraham's (Gen 15:6)**
- Scripture: Genesis 15:6
- 1C Reality: Abraham's tsedaqah is YHWH's declaration (va-yachshveha lo = "He reckoned it to him"), not earned by heroic deeds; he'emin = relational trust, not moral achievement
- Key Evidence: Guan Gong's apotheosis (human -> god) inverts the Genesis logic where a powerless old man trusts YHWH's promise

## Hinduism (hindu-theology)

**Claim 1: Virgin birth parallel (Krishna = Jesus)**
- Scripture: Isaiah 7:14
- 1C Reality: almah = young woman, not virgin (betulah); Devaki (Krishna's mother) was married with 7 previous children; Bhagavata Purana dates to 9-10C CE
- Key Evidence: ha-almah (definite article) = a specific known woman present at the scene; context is Syro-Ephraimite crisis (~735 BCE)

**Claim 2: John 10:30 "I and the Father are one" = Atman is Brahman**
- Scripture: John 10:30-36
- 1C Reality: Greek hen (neuter "one") rules out personal identity (masculine heis would mean that); whether hen indicates shared purpose, shared nature, or both is debated among scholars. Jesus' own defense cites Psalm 82:6 using kal va-chomer -- lowering the claim to Torah-acceptable range
- Key Evidence: Shema (Deut 6:4) = strict monotheism; shaliach principle ("agent = sender") explains unity as agency, not monism

**Claim 3: John 14:6 = Vedic "truth is one" (Ekam Sat)**
- Scripture: John 14:6
- 1C Reality: "No one comes to the Father except through me" = exclusive agency claim, opposite of Rig Veda 1.164.46 pluralism
- Key Evidence: derekh/emet/chayyim map to Torah concepts (path of Torah, YHWH's faithfulness, life from Torah); avatar (divine descent) vs. mashiach (human anointed by God)

## Islam (islamic-theology)

**Claim 1: Bible has been corrupted (Tahrif)**
- 1C Reality: Dead Sea Scrolls (3C BCE - 1C CE) confirm textual stability; 5,800 Greek NT MSS across 3 continents; Josephus (Contra Apionem 1.42) records Jews would die rather than alter scripture
- Key Evidence: tahrif bi-al-lafz (textual corruption) first proposed by Ibn Hazm (11C), not based on manuscript evidence

**Claim 2: Deuteronomy 18:18 "from their brothers" = Ishmaelites (Muhammad)**
- Scripture: Deuteronomy 18:15-18
- 1C Reality: miqerev acheihem = "from among their brothers" = Israelite kinsmen; same structure in Deut 17:15 explicitly contrasts "brothers" with "foreigner" (ish nokhri); Deut 18:1-2 uses identical phrase for Levites among tribes
- Key Evidence: 4QTestimonia (1C BCE) applies this to an Israelite prophet; Acts 3:22 and 7:37 apply it to Jesus (an Israelite)

**Claim 3: Parakletos (John 14:16) was originally Periklutos = Ahmad/Muhammad**
- Scripture: John 14:16-17, 26
- 1C Reality: Zero MSS (of 5,800) read periklutos; different roots (para+kaleo vs. peri+klyo); 14:26 defines parakletos = "Holy Spirit" (to pneuma to hagion); 14:17 "world cannot see him" -- Muhammad was visible
- Key Evidence: P66 (~150 CE), Sinaiticus, Vaticanus all read parakletos; Jewish peraklit in Mishnah Avot 4:11 = advocate/defender

## Japanese Buddhism (japanese-buddhism)

**Claim 1: Pure Land nembutsu = Romans 10:13 "call on the name"**
- Scripture: Romans 10:13 (quoting Joel 3:5 / 2:32)
- 1C Reality: qara' b'shem YHWH = covenant invocation (acknowledging YHWH as sole God + Torah obedience), not mantra recitation; Joel 3:5 includes YHWH also calling (bilateral)
- Key Evidence: Shema prerequisite; Rom 10:14 chain: believe -> hear -> proclaim -> be sent; nembutsu's "name-power" (hongan-riki) vs. covenant relationship

**Claim 2: Nichiren chanting = Acts 4:12 "no other name"**
- Scripture: Acts 4:12
- 1C Reality: "Name" = authority of a specific historical person (Nazarene Yeshua, crucified, resurrected); Peter speaks before Sanhedrin citing Ps 118:22; shem = authorization, not syllabic power
- Key Evidence: Namu Myoho Renge Kyo = title of a sutra (cosmic law); Acts 4:12 points to historical person + YHWH's action in history

**Claim 3: Zen koan = Jesus' parables (mashal)**
- Scripture: Matthew 13:10-13, Ezekiel 17:2
- 1C Reality: mashal has explicit referents -- Jesus himself explains (Matt 13:18-23); Nathan says "you are the man" (2 Sam 12:7); purpose is to teach Torah, not break conceptual thought
- Key Evidence: Koan = transcend language; mashal = deepen understanding through language; "so you can do it" (la'asoto, Deut 30:14)

## Jehovah's Witnesses (jw-theology)

**Claim 1: John 1:1 "the Word was a god"**
- Scripture: John 1:1c (kai theos en ho logos)
- 1C Reality: Anarthrous predicate nominative before verb = qualitative (Colwell 1933, Harner 1973) -- "the Word had the nature of God"; "a god" introduces polytheism incompatible with Shema
- Key Evidence: John 20:28 Thomas says ho theos mou (with article); John 8:58 ego eimi echoes Exod 3:14 ehyeh; memra/hokhmah tradition

**Claim 2: Jesus = Archangel Michael**
- Scripture: Daniel 10:13, 1 Thessalonians 4:16
- 1C Reality: Dan 10:13 achad hasarim harishonim = "one of the chief princes" (one among many); 1 Thess 4:16 en phone archangelou = "with" archangel's voice (accompaniment, not identity); Hebrews 1:5-8 argues Son is higher than all angels
- Key Evidence: Jude 1:9 Michael "did not dare" rebuke the devil; Jesus commands demons with full authority (Mark 1:25); Bible never equates Jesus = Michael

**Claim 3: Holy Spirit is impersonal force**
- Scripture: Genesis 1:2
- 1C Reality: Acts 5:3-4 equates lying to Spirit = lying to God (you cannot deceive a force); Rom 8:26-27 Spirit "intercedes" and has phronema (mindset); Eph 4:30 Spirit can be "grieved"
- Key Evidence: merachefet (Gen 1:2) = volitional hovering (like eagle over young, Deut 32:11); all attributed actions require personhood

**Claim 4: Jesus died on a stake, not a cross**
- Scripture: Deuteronomy 21:22 (etz = wood/tree)
- 1C Reality: stauros in 1C Roman context = various shapes including crossbeam (Seneca); John 20:25 helon (nails, plural) implies hands nailed separately; Giv'at ha-Mivtar archaeology (1968) shows crucifixion with structure
- Key Evidence: P66/P75 staurogram (~200 CE) depicts figure on cross; shape doesn't affect theology (qillat Elohim applies regardless), but evidence favors crossbeam

## Local Church / Watchman Nee-Witness Lee (local-church)

**Claim 1: Deification (theosis) -- humans become God in life and nature**
- Scripture: 2 Peter 1:4, 2 Corinthians 3:18
- 1C Reality: koinonoi theias physeos = "sharers in divine nature" = ethical participation, not ontological fusion; metamorphoumetha (passive voice) = being changed by God's kavod, not becoming God
- Key Evidence: tzelem Elohim (Gen 1:26-27) = humans reflect God's image; imitatio Dei (Deut 28:9) = walk in His ways; Isa 43:10 "before me no god was formed" = Creator/creature distinction absolute; theosis as doctrine begins with Irenaeus (2C)

**Claim 2: Pray-reading (repeating scripture as prayer)**
- Scripture: Ephesians 6:17-18
- 1C Reality: "Sword of the Spirit = word of God" is military metaphor in armor passage; rhema = specific spoken word in context; 1C Jewish engagement with Torah involved study, debate, memorization -- not repetitive chanting
- Key Evidence: Shema practice = recitation + meditation + teaching; no 1C parallel to "pray-reading" methodology

## Marketplace / Seven Mountain Theology (marketplace-theology)

**Claim: Christians must "occupy" seven spheres of society (Isaiah 2:2, Proverbs 22:29, Genesis 1:28)**
- Scripture: Isaiah 2:2, Proverbs 22:29, Genesis 1:28
- 1C Reality: Isa 2:2 har beit-YHWH = one specific mountain (Zion); direction is nations flowing toward Zion, not believers going out to conquer; Prov 22:29 mahir = skilled craftsman who serves kings, not becomes one; Gen 1:28 radah = stewardship of animals/earth within garden, not dominion over social institutions
- Key Evidence: Seven Mountain Mandate coined 1975 (Bright/Cunningham); heharim = literal mountains in prophetic idiom; yityatzav = "stand to serve," not "rule"

## Mashiach Concept (mashiach-concept)

**Claim: "Messiah" = divine second person of Trinity**
- Scripture: Psalm 2:2, Isaiah 11:1-5, Daniel 7:13
- 1C Reality: mashiach = "anointed one" (functional title applied to priests, kings, even Cyrus in Isa 45:1); 1C expectations were diverse: Davidic military king, priestly messiah (Qumran), prophet like Moses
- Key Evidence: Acts 1:6 disciples ask about restoring Israel's political kingdom; Qumran 1QS 9:11 expects "messiahs of Aaron and Israel" (plural, human); mashiach never means "God incarnate" in Tanakh

## Matthew 5:17 / Torah Continuity (matthew-5-17-torah)

**Claim: "Fulfill" means Jesus completed the Law so Christians needn't keep it**
- Scripture: Matthew 5:17-20
- 1C Reality: plerOsai corresponds to Hebrew qiyyem = "establish, correctly interpret"; opposite is katalysai/batel = "nullify by wrong interpretation"; Jesus intensifies Torah (5:21ff), not abolishes it
- Key Evidence: iota = yod (smallest Hebrew letter); keraia = tagin (decorative strokes); v.19 "whoever does and teaches" = practice + instruction; dikaiosyne = tsedaqah (covenant righteousness, not forensic justification)

## Modern Rabbinic Judaism (modern-judaism)

**Claim 1: Isaiah 53 "Suffering Servant" = Israel as a nation**
- Scripture: Isaiah 52:13-53:12
- 1C Reality: National reading first appears with Rashi (~1040-1105 CE); Targum Jonathan explicitly says "my servant the Messiah" (avdi meshicha) at 52:13; Talmud Sanhedrin 98b links Isa 53:4 to a suffering Messiah
- Key Evidence: Isaiah 49:5-6 distinguishes the servant from Israel ("to bring Jacob back"); 53:8 "for my people's transgression" = servant is not the people

**Claim 2: Echad (Deut 6:4) = absolute mathematical singularity ruling out Jesus**
- Scripture: Deuteronomy 6:4
- 1C Reality: echad can mean "unified one" (Gen 2:24 basar echad, "one flesh"); "Two Powers in Heaven" (shtei reshuyot) was accepted in 1C Judaism before being declared heresy in 2C (Segal, 1977); Daniel 7:13 "Son of Man" receives palach (worship reserved for God)
- Key Evidence: Narrowing echad to mathematical singularity is post-2C anti-Christian polemic, not 1C Jewish theology

## Mormonism / LDS (mormon-theology)

**Claim 1: Baptism for the dead (1 Corinthians 15:29)**
- Scripture: 1 Corinthians 15:29
- 1C Reality: Paul uses 3rd person "they" (not "we"); reductio ad absurdum argument for resurrection, not endorsement; no other NT passage teaches or practices proxy baptism
- Key Evidence: hyper (for/on behalf of/because of) has broad semantic range; Corinth was chaotic (Paul corrects them throughout); over 40 scholarly interpretations exist

**Claim 2: God was once human; humans become gods (exaltation)**
- Scripture: (No direct biblical text)
